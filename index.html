FAQ

Common questions.

What's the difference between the two packages?
The One-Pager is a tangible deliverable - a professional personal brand page that goes live on the internet. BrandxAI is a strategic process - together with AI, we discover your brand voice, values, positioning, and audience. One gives you a page, the other gives you clarity about your entire brand. Many people start with BrandxAI and then get the One-Pager built on top of it.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
None at all. I handle all the technical work. Your brand page is built in clean HTML - no complicated platforms, no plugins to update, no databases to manage. You get a walkthrough of everything so you understand how to make small edits yourself if you want to.
Is my page secure?
Yes. Your page is static HTML hosted on Vercel with free SSL (HTTPS). Because there's no server-side code, no database, and no login system, there's essentially nothing to hack. It's one of the most secure approaches to having a website. Your contact form runs through a secure third-party service, so no data is stored on the page itself.
Where is my page hosted?
Your code lives on GitHub (version-controlled, you own it) and the page is served by Vercel - both are free. Vercel provides automatic HTTPS, global CDN, and instant deploys. If you ever want to move it somewhere else, you can - it's just an HTML file.
How fast can I get my page live?
A personal brand one-pager is typically delivered in 2-3 days. The BrandxAI brand discovery process takes about a week, depending on your availability for the guided sessions.
Can I add a custom domain?
Yes. Vercel supports custom domains for free. I can help you set up yourname.com or any domain you own. Until then, your page is live on a clean .vercel.app URL that you can share right away.
What do I get with the BrandxAI package?
You get a complete brand strategy document that includes your brand voice and tone, core values, brand story, target audience profiles, market positioning, visual identity direction (colors, fonts, mood), and actionable next steps. Think of it as the blueprint for everything you do online - from social media to your website to how you introduce yourself.
Can I update my page after it's live?
Absolutely. Your page is on GitHub, so you can edit the HTML directly through the browser - no special software needed. When you save changes, Vercel automatically deploys the update within seconds. I walk you through this process during handover.
